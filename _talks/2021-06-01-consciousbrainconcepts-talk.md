Abstract and talk presented at the ConsciousBrainConcepts Annual Workshop (2021), an event organised in part by the author. The talk addressed the validity and interpretation of EEG-based complexity measures as indices of consciousness, drawing on systematic review findings and empirical studies from the Brain Signalling Group.
